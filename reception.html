Reception Hall

Love is the best excuse to throw a party

Every couple wants their wedding day to be fun and free of stress. You hope your big day will be exactly the way you’ve planned. Maybe there’s a family tradition passed down from generations you want to keep, or cultures brought together in a special way. You may want to break a few rules or combine ideas to make it your own. From the linens, menu and seating chart, down to the flowers and other personal touches, We are here to help you coordinate the details of your wedding reception.

Dinner Menus

Have a taste for light hors d’oeuvres? Maybe a five-course formal dinner? Whatever you have in mind, our expert catering teams work with you to plan a completely customized menu. Enjoy incredible dining options whether your event calls for traditional sit-down service or a more relaxed atmosphere.

Reception Tips

A little variety on the reception menu goes a long way. Consider adding an innovative vegetarian or vegan option for guests with special dietary needs. And did you know that you could save money by booking your wedding during off-peak times? The guest list can be flexible, too. Our helpful resources and event planning team make it easy to coordinate the details – so you can focus on enjoying your celebration.

Hotel Accommodations

With a Personalized Group Web Page, your guests find details and book rooms from your own online meeting hub. Use Group Value Dates to search for special rates when you book multiple guest rooms for your event.
